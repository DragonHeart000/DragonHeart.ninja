Lore

There has not been many recent terror attacks for at least a few years but now all of a sudden this cult has taken it upon themselves to destroy this seemingly innocent convenience store, but is it truly that innocent?

The police have to do their job and protect this civilian building but while doing so have run into a few strange things most important of which being a small back room with the Phoenix's banner and a relay station! Could this be set up by the perpetrators or is the store an intelligence outpost for the Phoenix?
​
The police have a mission and their current objective is to exterminate the threat and ask questions later... let's hope that does not end up being a mistake.

History

De_Convenience is an alteration of DragonHeart000's first map ever. Technically 2v2_Convenience was his first map but later on he took that map and remade it into a full 5v5 Defuse map. The map got very far into development and many hours went into it until DragonHeart000 lost some of the textures from the map and couldn't get a decompiler to run on the maps .bsp file. So he found it as a fine time to go and set off to work on bigger projects and laid this project to rest for the time being.
